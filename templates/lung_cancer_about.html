Lung Cancer information



OVERVIEW

Lung cancer is a cancer that begins in the lungs and most often occurs in people who smoke.

SYMPTOMPS

  • Pain in the chest or rib
  • Cough can be chronic, dry, with phlegm, or with blood
  • Frequent respiratory infections, shortness of breath or wheezing
  • Fatigue or loss of appetite
  • Common hoarseness, swollen lymph nodes, weakness, or weight loss
